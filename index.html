What we see here is a collective of work, made by over 93 papers and online shares, from over 300 HCI scientist who have been thinking about the challenge of text input on-the-go, and for XR over decades. This vault is actively curated in collaboration between researchers of Arizona State University, University of Birmingham, Københavns Universitet and Google. The vault team includes: Prof. H. Seifi, Prof. M. Di Luca, Dr. A. Bhatia, Dr. D. Karim, Dr. E.J. Gonzalez, S. Macgregor, J.A. Fotso-Puepi, Prof. K. Ahuja and Dr. M. Gonzalez-Franco.
